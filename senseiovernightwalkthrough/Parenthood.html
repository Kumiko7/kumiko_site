Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu visits the bookstore, where he encounters Nokana and Tora. The group discusses Nokana’s large family and her lack of materialism, followed by Tora’s insistence on taking Nokana shopping to update her wardrobe, despite Nokana’s reluctance. Janu, Tora, and Nokana decide to head to the plaza, where they are joined by Sora.

Upon arriving at the plaza, the group visits a luxury boutique. Tora directs Nokana into a dressing room to try on lingerie, intending to boost her confidence and "sexify" her image. Janu, initially waiting outside with Sora, is eventually called into the dressing room by Sora to provide an outside opinion on Nokana's appearance.

Afterward, the group proceeds to a salon for a makeover, where they discuss hairstyles. A conversation ensues involving Tami, Tora’s sister, regarding the shopping trip. The event concludes after a photoshoot in the boutique, where it is revealed that Tora used Tami’s credit card without permission to pay for the clothes, leading to a brief argument between the sisters that is diffused by Nokana. Janu observes that while the makeover was not as transformative as others, it successfully boosted Nokana's appearance, and he expresses a lingering interest in seeing her in the new attire again.
