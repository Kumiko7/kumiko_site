During a Thursday P.E. lesson, Janu enters the male changing room, intending to change into more appropriate clothing for the class. He discovers Anii Mai inside, who is in a state of undress and searching frantically through her bag. A tense confrontation ensues, during which Anii reacts with extreme hostility and physical aggression toward Janu, accusing him of being a creep for "sneaking up" on her.

Janu defends his presence by noting the signage on the door and asserting his right to use the male changing room, while pointing out that Anii’s presence in the male facility is the actual anomaly. Meanwhile, the perspective shifts to Dayai and Tuli, who discuss Anii's reclusive behavior during P.E. sessions. Nirima interrupts to reveal that Anii has consistently used the male changing room since transferring to the school, implying that she and Janu are likely alone together. Nirima also makes pointed, unsettling comments about the nature of Janu’s attention toward the students, leaving Dayai feeling unsettled.

Back in the changing room, the argument continues until Janu attempts to de-escalate by offering to change on the other side of the room. Despite his attempts to be cordial, he inadvertently heightens the tension by calling Anii "cute." The scene concludes with a standoff as Janu retreats to his side of the room, leaving Anii flustered and the atmosphere charged with unresolved conflict.
