Summary (Click to reveal)

Following a brief interruption from Janu, Juna continues her conversation with Eruka and Sora while lying with them in bed. The girls press Juna for details about Janu’s life. When the topic of Janu's recent illness arises, Juna defends his caretaker, Dayai, explaining that her protective behavior stems from her love for him. The conversation shifts to a get-well-soon card made by their classmate, Mori, which leads to a brief, awkward confusion regarding Phaigon dialect.

The discussion then turns to Dayai and Juna’s relationship. Sora shares a theory that Dayai only recently remembered Juna after Janu moved to the city, which Juna dismisses as wild speculation. Kio and Chinaka join the group, and Sora attempts to alleviate the tension by listing nice traits about everyone in their class. The group eventually decides to move the gathering to Eruka's room, which they decide will be a "cozy" rather than cramped space, before they depart to get some rest.
