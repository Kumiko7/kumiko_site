Janu visits a gym in an effort to improve his fitness after being teased for his lack of athleticism. Upon arrival, he encounters Anii, who immediately attempts to discourage him from leaving, teasing him as a "premature quitter." Their interaction quickly evolves into a playful power struggle, with Anii asserting her role as both a teacher and his new personal trainer. Despite Janu’s initial resistance, Anii remains persistent, claiming he will eventually feel compelled to return to the gym. The scene concludes with Janu leaving the gym for the moment, but the encounter leaves him with the impression that his fitness journey under Anii's guidance is now inevitable.
