Summary (Click to reveal)

Hana introduces a century-old, high-alcohol-content wine imported from Italy, aiming to make the sleepover more exciting. While Tuli and Tora initially express reservations about drinking, they are eventually persuaded to join Hana and Dayai in consuming the strong beverage. As they become heavily intoxicated, the girls begin gossiping and baring their personal struggles. Tora voices frustration over her sister Tami’s condescending behavior and questions about her virginity, while Tuli laments her own lack of romantic experience.

When Hana reveals her own past sexual experience, she calls the others "nerds" for being "kiss virgins." Seeking to prove her friends wrong, Hana challenges them to kiss. Although Tuli quickly runs off to be sick, Hana and Tora later move into Janu’s room while he is deeply asleep. Claiming that the best way to wake a man is through pleasure, Hana initiates a sexual act on the sleeping Janu. Tora initially observes but ultimately opts to leave the room. Hana continues the act to completion, marveling at the experience and deciding to clean up afterward, while telling herself to pretend the encounter never happened as she prepares for the following morning.
