Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu walks along the beach, reflecting on his complex state of mind and the unpredictable nature of his personal life, before encountering Azula, a bartender he met the previous night. They engage in a candid conversation where Janu admits to his own lack of direction in life. The two discuss the aftermath of the previous night’s events involving Tami and Ryu; depending on earlier choices, Janu either confirms his participation in a sexual encounter with them or explains that he was simply helping a drunken Tami home.

As they continue to talk, Janu learns that Azula is 21 and finds her perspective on her job and life at the resort refreshing. The scene shifts as Zena and Eruka appear, leading to a reunion between Zena and Azula, who were previously "friends" at the resort. Zena’s presence and her comfortable, flirtatious behavior with Azula prompt Janu to realize the deep, unconventional connection they share. Eruka later joins Janu for a quiet moment, where she comments on the tension-breaking effect of the morning's activities and expresses her comfort around him. Their conversation is interrupted when Janu receives a voice note from Juna, requesting he meet her at the mall to discuss a matter of importance, prompting Janu to leave Eruka behind and head to the meeting place.
