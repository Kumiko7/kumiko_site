Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu is in his classroom after school, lamenting the end of the school year and his lack of quality time with certain students. His niece, Dayai, interrupts him, explaining that the Arts and Crafts club is closed for "baby-proofing," leaving her with nothing to do. Despite some initial bickering and Janu’s teasing about her trying to avoid being a "loser," they agree to have a tutor session, though Dayai admits she has no interest in actual studying and simply wants to hang out.

They wander the school to find a private spot, eventually settling on a secluded bench. During their conversation, Dayai confesses that she felt excluded and "in the way" because of the secret-keeping between Janu and Juna, which prompted her to move into the dorms. She expresses remorse for her behavior and a desire to make amends. Janu notes that Juna seems to be struggling with family issues and encourages Dayai to reconcile with her.

Dayai invites Janu to dinner that Saturday, and they discuss their respective relationships with Juna and Tora. The event concludes with Dayai sending Janu a text message asking him to help get Juna out of their shared apartment that Saturday so she can retrieve some belongings without an awkward encounter. Janu agrees to the request, and the two reaffirm their bond. Depending on the player's history with the character Hana, the scene either ends with a discussion about checking in on Tora or a brief moment of Janu teasing Dayai about her desire to reconcile with her family.
