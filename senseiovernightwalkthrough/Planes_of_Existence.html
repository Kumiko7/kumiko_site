Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu finds Oki looking out a window, seemingly distracted. Oki expresses an unusual sense of restlessness and asks Janu to accompany her to a secluded, "undiscovered" garden path she recently found. Upon arriving at the tranquil location, Oki explains that she believes this place holds a special connection to the spiritual plane.

Oki guides Janu through a meditation exercise intended to help them achieve inner peace and move beyond the conflict they feel within themselves. She asks Janu if he trusts her, and they engage in a physical touch exercise, placing hands on each other's chests. Oki then directs Janu to push her lightly on her mark, triggering a disorienting, bright light.

Both characters experience a shift in perception as they detach from their physical bodies, transitioning into a spiritual plane of existence where they can see their own physical forms sitting nearby. Oki explains that while they are currently existing in both the physical and spiritual worlds, it is a rare and difficult feat for most people. She admits she was initially nervous to share this with Janu but now feels she can trust him. Their conversation is interrupted by a high-pitched noise, which Oki identifies as a sign that someone is approaching their physical bodies.

Oki guides them back to reality, where they are greeted by Omina. Omina is confused by their state and questions why they were touching, but the interaction remains relatively mundane as Omina invites Oki and Janu to join her for dinner (though Janu declines). After Omina leaves, Janu reflects on the new, profound questions raised by this experience, realizing that Oki is a significant mystery who shares his unique ability to traverse different planes of existence. He concludes that while this journey creates more questions than answers, he is glad to be exploring his own identity alongside her.
