Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu wakes up early on Saturday morning and heads to the public library, where he encounters his student, Odessa. They share a conversation about Odessa’s social anxieties, her desire for routine, and her admiration for her classmate Rina. Odessa opens up about her struggle to develop a personality beyond her "surface-level" traits, while Janu expresses an interest in learning more about her.

After leaving the library, Janu heads to the plaza, where he encounters his students Rei and Yuka. Rei is embarrassed by Janu's presence, while Yuka is more forward, pressuring Janu to engage in a roleplay scenario where he acts as her boyfriend. Yuka attempts to make him feel jealous regarding his girlfriend, Eruka, and challenges him to buy lingerie for her to model. Janu plays along with the roleplay to navigate Yuka's passive-aggressiveness, ultimately rejecting her advances while using the interaction to analyze her competitive nature and complex feelings. He concludes the event by deciding to buy the lingerie for Eruka, leaving Yuka unsettled by the encounter.
