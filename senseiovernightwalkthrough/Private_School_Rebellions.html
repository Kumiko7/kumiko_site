Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu holds a tutor session with Zena, a new student from England, and finds himself caught off guard by her formal demeanor. As they speak, Zena reveals her background, describing her old private school as a restrictive, surveillance-heavy environment where she rebelled against conformism. She expresses a newfound sense of freedom at her current school and, after Janu gives her permission, enthusiastically explores the act of cursing in class.

Determining that Zena is academically advanced and doesn't truly require tutoring, Janu agrees to take her on a tour of the school instead. During the tour, Zena becomes physically affectionate, linking her arm with Janu's, which catches the attention of Dayai. Dayai initially reacts with jealousy upon seeing Zena monopolizing Janu, but her attitude shifts when Zena begins massaging her shoulders, leading to a surprisingly positive interaction between the two girls. Janu ultimately leaves them to continue their conversation while he heads to the school gates, noting Zena's bold personality and the unexpected dynamic she has established.
