Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu receives a call from Anii, who informs him that Roni is at the gym in an extremely volatile state after her failed apology attempt with Mori the previous night. Upon arriving, Janu finds Roni relentlessly beating a punching bag, having cleared the gym of other patrons. Zena is also present, expressing a disturbed form of fascination and masochistic attraction toward Roni’s aggression.

Janu eventually gets Roni to stop and take a break. Roni confesses her deep frustration and sense of failure, admitting that her attempt to make amends with Mori fell apart because she could not face the magnitude of her past wrongdoings. She expresses a desire to keep up her defensive "barrier" of anger, as she is not yet ready to process the guilt of her actions. Janu promises to remain her anchor and offers his continued support without pressuring her to make further amends until she is ready. The event concludes with Roni acknowledging Janu’s loyalty and the trio leaving the gym, with Roni still dealing with the complex aftermath of her confrontation with Mori.
