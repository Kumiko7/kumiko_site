Summary (Click to reveal)

In the courtyard, Janu encounters Zena, a student who recently transferred from England. Zena is taking photographs and jokingly asks if Janu has any room for her in his living arrangements. Their conversation takes a serious turn when Zena reveals she remembers Janu from a previous private encounter with Eruka. She assures Janu that she intends to keep their secret, expressing a surprisingly open-minded perspective on student-teacher relationships.

As they speak, Chinaka arrives and introduces herself as Janu’s "unofficial secretary." Zena identifies herself as the cousin of Hana, a girl Janu already knows. Zena reveals that she has been assigned to Janu’s class, which Chinaka excitedly frames as a growing "harem." When the group moves to the main school building, they are met by an irate Hana, who is upset that Zena ignored her scripted class introduction.

The group enters the classroom, where the other students are immediately struck by Zena's appearance. Hana attempts to deliver a scripted, lengthy introduction, but Zena rebels and speaks for herself. The tension escalates when Hana and Chinaka engage in a heated, mocking argument. The scene descends into chaos when Zena encourages Chinaka to physically attack Hana, resulting in a brief fight between the two. Janu eventually intervenes to break up the scuffle, noting that Zena seems to have integrated into his "class of misfits" quite effectively. He remains confident that Zena will uphold her promise to keep his secret with Eruka.
