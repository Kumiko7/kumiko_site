Janu meets Kio at a jazz café, where he notices her displaying signs of guilt and regret following a recent discussion with Eruka. Kio invites Janu to her home after closing time to discuss the matter privately, assuring him that her parents will be out working long hours.

Upon arriving at her room, Kio reveals her true intention: she wants to get high and is looking for a companion to reach a state of euphoria with her. Janu is initially hesitant, concerned about the potential smell and his own comfort, but he is eventually presented with the choice to either join her or decline.

If the player chooses to decline, Janu stays briefly to talk before leaving, resulting in a slightly awkward departure where he feels the lingering effects of the environment. If the player chooses to get high, the pair spends two hours in an intoxicated state, leading to a surreal and bizarre conversation that includes an encounter with Kio’s "Rastafarian rabbit" stuffed animal, Ruben. The event concludes with Janu leaving Kio’s home after their shared experience, marking a shift in their dynamic.
