    Summary (Click to reveal)

    Janu visits the sports track looking for Tuli following her chaotic night out. He encounters Rina, a first-year student and Tuli’s new trainee, who reveals that Tuli has been acting strangely and has locked herself in the storage room. Concerned about Tuli’s claustrophobia and history of trauma, Janu pries the door open and finds her in a state of distress, with chipped, bloody fingernails from clawing at the walls.

    As Tuli slips into a dissociative, guilt-ridden trance, the narrative reveals traumatic diary entries detailing her upbringing: her parents’ neglect, their intense pressure to excel as athletes, and the cycle of physical and psychological abuse she suffered whenever she failed to meet their impossible standards. Trapped in these memories, Tuli repeats that she is an "embarrassment" and believes she deserves her punishments. Unable to find the right words to comfort her, Janu embraces Tuli to help ground her, holding her firmly as she panics and begs for forgiveness, vowing not to let go until she begins to process her pain.
