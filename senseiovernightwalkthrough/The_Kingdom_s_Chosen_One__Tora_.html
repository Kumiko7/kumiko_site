Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu experiences a dream set within a lonely, rhythmic train carriage that evokes feelings of nostalgia and past trauma. Reflecting on his transition from a solitary youth to someone who now seeks connection, Janu questions his morality, ultimately comforting himself with the belief that his life is a "gifted miracle."

The dream sequence concludes abruptly as the train arrives at a station, where an array of mysterious items appears before Janu. He is then awakened by his maid, Dayai, who is now manifesting within this dream world. Dayai adopts a submissive persona, addressing Janu as "Master" and explaining that they are in the kingdom of Galanthus. She informs Janu that he has been tasked with a heroic quest: he must wake Princess Tora from a deep slumber, with Noble Knights awaiting his arrival in the town square. Following this briefing, Janu transitions to his home environment, where he begins interacting with Dayai and preparing to embark on his quest by seeking out the Knights and necessary equipment.
