Janu calls Eruka for a private study session at the school’s study room, seeking a reprieve from recent world-hopping tensions. Their meeting is interrupted by Eruka's friend Oki, who arrives to prevent what she views as Janu grooming Eruka into his harem. Despite Oki's hostility, Eruka uses emotional manipulation to force an intervention, demanding that Janu and Oki reconcile their falling out.

The three sit down to discuss the conflict. Oki accuses Janu of being a manipulative conqueror interested only in sexual gratification. During the argument, it is revealed that Oki is aware of a past encounter between Janu and Eruka at a love hotel. Oki remains unconvinced of Janu's sincerity and leaves the room, refusing to reconcile until Janu proves his character. After Oki’s departure, Janu and Eruka discuss the arrival of a new student, D'vala, who claims to have memories of a relationship with Janu. Janu insists he is a stranger to D'vala but pledges to help her find normalcy, while assuring Eruka that their bond remains unique. The scene concludes with the two finally proceeding with their intimate session.
