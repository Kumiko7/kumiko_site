Summary (Click to reveal)

After a period of avoiding formal study, Janu attempts to tutor Dayai. Dayai attempts to keep the session casual and lighthearted, but eventually nudges Janu to relocate the session to a private study room. Once there, it is revealed that Dayai has secretly prepared snacks, catching Janu off guard.

The two discuss their relationship and their time living together. During the conversation, Janu is prompted to discuss a recent trip to Okakri, allowing the player to choose whether Janu went with Eruka, went alone, or has yet to visit. As the interaction deepens, Janu reveals plans to return to their former hometown of Phaigon for the anniversary of their mother's death. Dayai, saddened to learn she was excluded from this information, insists on joining Janu to pay respects to her late sister. Janu agrees, and the two end the session on a more intimate note, sharing a game of Mario Kart, with Janu feeling relieved to have Dayai’s company for the upcoming trip.
