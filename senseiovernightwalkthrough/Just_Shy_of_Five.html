Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu visits Anii in the dorms, where he finds her studying. Despite their usual banter, Anii expresses a newfound interest in learning, mentioning that she enjoyed being tutored by Nirima and realized she actually likes studying. During their conversation, Anii introduces Janu to her new study buddy, Chinaka.

Chinaka is introduced as a student from Ms. Wanasha’s class who possesses a surprisingly intellectual interest in subjects like quantum theory. After a brief, prickly exchange where Chinaka mocks Nirima's appearance and social life, she and Janu bond over her hobby of cosplay. The conversation turns to physical comparisons between the girls, and Chinaka insists on measuring her height against Anii’s to settle a debate, using Janu as an adjudicator. The scene concludes with the group returning to their studies, though Janu leaves feeling intrigued by Chinaka’s depth and maturity, realizing he forgot to ask for the link to her online cosplay page.
