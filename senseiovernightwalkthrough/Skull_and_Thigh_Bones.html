Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu arrives at the music store to find Roni dealing with a difficult, impatient customer complaining about a delayed custom order. Janu intervenes by pretending to be Roni’s relative, creating a chaotic scene that forces the caller to hang up. Roni remains frustrated by her business struggles, including supplier issues and the burden of managing her own shop while trying to handle online requests.

As they converse, Roni mentions her recent viral exposure on social media, which has led to strange requests from people looking to purchase custom items paired with suggestive photos. The two discuss Janu’s personal relationships, with Roni teasing him about his reputation as a "harem king." Eventually, Janu agrees to accompany Roni on her scooter to a supply store to help her acquire food coloring.

Upon arriving, they encounter Juna, who helps Roni with her supplies. During the visit, the shop's lights fail, and they discover Hana is also shopping in the dark. Roni attempts to check on Hana, whose life has been in turmoil following recent public humiliation, but Hana rejects Roni’s attempt at kindness as insincere. Hana dismisses them with icy detachment, and after the lights return, she leaves the store, leaving Roni disappointed by her failed attempt at reconciliation.
