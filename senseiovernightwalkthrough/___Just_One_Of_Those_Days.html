Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu travels to school with his niece, Dayai, struggling with internal questions about her reality and whether he has truly returned to his own world. During the conversation, Dayai discusses their past, graduation, and her upcoming college plans, while also teasing Janu about his role as a teacher. Janu grapples with his guilt over missing the graduation ceremony and his complicated, non-platonic relationship with his niece.

After confirming his existence within the world, Janu notices that Juna has seemingly disappeared, with Dayai having no recollection of her. Upon arriving at the school, Janu is greeted by a new student, Aisha Enkruma, who is eager to start her first year. Aisha relays eccentric messages from her cousins, Zena and Hana, hinting at their awareness of Janu’s personal life. The event concludes with Janu preparing to manage his new class, 1B, while still navigating his unresolved, intimate connection with Dayai.
