Summary (Click to reveal)

Eruka surprises Janu in his office by appearing naked on his couch, a situation Janu notes could have been disastrous if his student Dayai had witnessed it instead. The two discuss the morning's events, with Janu observing that Eruka is attempting to be more daring, and they briefly touch upon Eruka’s natural appearance, which Janu recalls seeing in a dream. Following this encounter, the scene shifts to the school, where Kio and Sone discuss their spring breaks and their plans for the upcoming Student Council election. Meanwhile, Roni engages in a tense confrontation with Zena regarding a bet, successfully forcing Zena to pay her by threatening to expose their agreement to the class. Elsewhere, Hana and Nokana debate the future of their book club, with Hana proposing seasonal literature themes while Nokana pushes to recruit new members. The scene concludes with Janu struggling with intense feelings of alienation and imposter syndrome. Despite Dayai’s attempts to convince their classmates that Janu is simply tired, Janu suffers a mental breakdown, eventually dismissing his class for the morning and retreating, feeling he does not belong in this world.
