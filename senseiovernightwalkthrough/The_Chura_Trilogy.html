Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu calls Tami at the salon, but Tora answers and informs him that Tami is busy speaking with their mother, Tanya, in the back. Tora is visibly irritated and cold toward Janu, annoyed that he only reached out to see her sister. When Janu arrives at the salon, he finds Tora in a sour mood. Tami eventually emerges and forces Janu and Tora to join a video call with their mother, who is currently in California.

During the call, Tanya Chura exhibits flirtatious and inappropriate behavior toward Janu, embarrassing Tora and leaving Tami somewhat stunned. Tanya criticizes her daughters' life choices, leading to a heated argument where Tora accuses her mother of abandoning them to manage the businesses alone. Tora ultimately storms out of the salon in anger. Left alone with Janu and Tami, Tanya continues to treat Janu with flirtatious curiosity and eventually recounts an uncomfortable story about Tami’s sexual past, leaving Tami deeply humiliated. The event concludes with Janu leaving the salon, having navigated a tense and dysfunctional family dynamic between the Chura women.
