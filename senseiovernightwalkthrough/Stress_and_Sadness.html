Janu receives a phone call from Tami’s younger sister, Tora, who is worried about Tami’s uncharacteristic behavior following a recent, embarrassing interaction with their mother. Tami confesses that she is overwhelmed by the pressure of managing two failing businesses, the burden of raising Tora, and the emotional toll of her mother’s recent visit.
