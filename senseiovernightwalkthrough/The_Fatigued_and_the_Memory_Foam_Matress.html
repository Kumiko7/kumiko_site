Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu arrives at Sora’s dorm room, where he is playfully teased about maintaining her clean rugs before getting a tour of her space. Sora goes to the study room to change into new pajamas, leaving Janu alone to examine her belongings, including a cryptic poem. When she returns, she reveals she is self-conscious about her appearance without makeup, displaying dark circles under her eyes that she insists are a result of her political campaign stress. Janu questions her honesty, suspecting deeper issues, but Sora deflects his concern and shifts the focus to watching anime together.

As they bond over the show, Sora opens up about her loneliness and her conflicted relationship with her adoptive parents, explaining that she moved into the dorms to seek independence. Despite her bravado, she admits to having chronic sleeping difficulties. Sora eventually invites Janu to stay the night, suggesting that his presence might help her finally get some rest. Janu agrees, and they spend the night sharing her bed. In the morning, Janu discovers that Sora has already left for the day, leaving behind a note, a cup of coffee, and a playful jab about his snoring.
