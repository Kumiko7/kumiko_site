Summary (Click to reveal)

Following the intense gym session, Anii and Chinaka prepare for a tennis match, trading verbal barbs about their skills and appearance. They are interrupted by Sora and Eruka, who invite the group to a new boba tea shop. While Janu initially planned to watch the tennis match, he decides to join the girls for the discounted boba, leaving Anii and Chinaka behind.

After the others depart, Anii’s mood shifts to a melancholic state, prompting Chinaka to halt the tennis plans to address Anii's underlying emotional distress. Anii reveals her deep-seated insecurities regarding her appearance and her confusion over her burgeoning, undefined romantic feelings for Janu. She struggles with the perception that she is a "tomboy" who cannot compete with the conventional attractiveness of girls like Eruka or Sora. Chinaka offers supportive advice, affirming Anii's unique qualities and suggesting that her authentic self is exactly what draws Janu to her. The scene concludes with the two friends setting aside their insecurities, renewing their determination to play their tennis match, and reaffirming their bond.
