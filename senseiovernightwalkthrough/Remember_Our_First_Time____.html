    Following the earlier gallery confrontation, Janu joins D'vala in a dusty school storage room under the pretense of retrieving gym supplies. D'vala manipulates the situation to create an intimate encounter, eventually causing them to fall onto gym mats together.

    D'vala confronts Janu about his stoic, distant demeanor, pointing out that he cannot hide his physical attraction to her. As they reminisce about their past relationship, D'vala expresses a deep desire to "start over" and rekindle their love in this new reality, acknowledging that while their current circumstances are complicated, she views their connection as a necessary refuge. Janu, struggling with his own conflicted feelings and the surreal nature of their shared history, admits he is unable to stay away from her.

    The scene transitions into a sexual encounter, during which D'vala explicitly frames their intimacy as a way to "reclaim" their bond, asking Janu to be her first again. Janu agrees to try and stop pushing her away. As they engage, D'vala professes her love and demands that Janu finish inside her, symbolizing a commitment to their future. Following the act, Janu reflects on the lingering emotional distance between them, yet finds himself internally committed to continuing this "twisted" dynamic, acknowledging a mutual desire for their pursuit of one another to continue.
