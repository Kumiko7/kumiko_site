    Summary (Click to reveal)

    Janu finds himself in an alleyway, questioning Mori about why she keeps saving his life despite their hostile dynamic. Mori expresses that her actions are against her better judgment, viewing Janu as a death-seeking individual, while Janu hints at a darker, subconscious motivation for her behavior. As their interaction intensifies, Janu experiences a psychological shift, appearing to lose control of his persona and exhibiting unnerving insight into Mori’s secret life, including her past, her vulnerabilities, and her private interests. During this trance-like state, Janu corners Mori and briefly chokes her, claiming that she secretly enjoys the sensation of fear and vulnerability.

    Mori manages to snap Janu out of his episode, though he is left shaken and confused. Despite the danger, she refuses to let him walk away, following him to a high vantage point to demand answers. She confronts him about his relationship with her best friend, Nokana. Janu reveals that Nokana kissed him, shattering Mori’s composure. The event concludes with a emotionally devastated Mori weeping and confessing her intense hatred for Janu, leaving the situation unresolved as Janu returns home.
