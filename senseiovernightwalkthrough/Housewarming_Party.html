Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu arrives at Ryu Ranihori’s housewarming party, where Ryu greets him and Tami with excessive enthusiasm. A misunderstanding arises regarding why Janu was invited before Tami, leading Ryu to panic and offer Tami anything in her fridge as an apology. The atmosphere turns tense when Tami interrogates Ryu about her relationship with Janu, specifically questioning if they have been physically intimate. Janu responds with a flippant, exaggerated claim that they have been "fucking all day, every day," which briefly confuses the room.

The scene shifts to Wendy and Lina, who discuss the progress of Wendy's kitchen renovations and their strained, "realistic" friendship. Janu attempts to compliment Lina, which she interprets as disingenuous due to his established façade. Meanwhile, Roni pulls Janu aside to express concern over his well-being, referencing a previous panic attack he suffered. She demands an honest explanation for his recent erratic behavior, and Janu admits to her that he is struggling with his mental state. The event concludes with Janu leaving the party early, overwhelmed by a need for fresh air, as he reflects on his inability to process or accept the comfort offered by those around him.
