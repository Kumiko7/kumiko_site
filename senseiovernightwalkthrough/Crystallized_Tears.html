Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu witnesses an emotional confrontation between Roni and Sora by the pool. Sora, feeling guilty for her past behavior, apologizes for pushing Roni’s buttons and admits she treated Roni poorly because she wrongly assumed Roni was still a bully. Roni reveals the traumatic origin of her aggressive behavior, explaining that Sora had once exposed Roni's crush to their classmates, leading to widespread social ostracization. Overcome with remorse for her role in Roni's past suffering, Sora breaks down, insisting she deserved the pain she received. Roni comforts a distraught Sora, and the two agree to move forward by being civil, acknowledging their shared past as children.

After the encounter, if Janu is currently involved with Hana, he receives a notification from her and departs to investigate. Otherwise, he observes the social dynamics of the group before the scene concludes. Search parties led by Dayai and Tora eventually locate Tuli, who is found in a distressed state in a storage room where she had been playing a "test of courage" game with Nirima. The situation escalates into a heated confrontation between Tora and Nirima over the latter's thoughtless game, resulting in Dayai ordering Nirima to leave. Janu subsequently finds Nirima on the roof, where she reflects on her actions and the social friction she causes, culminating in her asking Janu whether he considers her a terrible person.
