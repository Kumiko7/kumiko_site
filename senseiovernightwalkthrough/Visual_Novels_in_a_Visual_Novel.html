Summary (Click to reveal)

Janu arrives at the common room to find Kio, Dayai, Nirima, Tuli, and Anii gathered for a gaming event. Kio explains that she became obsessed with a visual novel called *Lessons in Love* and has organized a competition among the students to see who can be the first to clear a notoriously difficult event where all the characters disappear. The winner is promised a slice of Kio's homemade cake.

As the girls begin playing, Janu engages in banter with Kio regarding her "addiction" to visual novels and other H-games. The students focus intently on their screens, with Nirima acting particularly competitive, though Tuli ultimately beats her to the goal, much to Nirima's frustration. Anii struggles significantly with the game, as she is preoccupied with reading the lore and gets lost in the gameplay. Kio eventually declares Tuli the winner and brings out the prize. The event concludes with Janu reflecting on his shared "horny degenerate" interests with Kio and noting that while their group activity is unconventional, it serves as a lighthearted diversion for everyone involved.
