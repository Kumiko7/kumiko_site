    Summary (Click to reveal)

    In this event, Janu confronts his pet birds, Dice and Spudge, after discovering they have learned to use text-to-speech software. The birds claim to be brothers and demand affection, which Janu finds unsettling given his desire for a normal life. Tensions rise as the birds mock Janu's mental state, leading him to threaten them with eviction—a threat he never actually follows through on.

    Dice reveals he has been feeling pressured by his sister, Dorothy, who wants him to return to the farm. He asks Janu to accompany him to a nearby minimart so he can meet her, promising to keep out of Janu’s way. Janu reluctantly agrees and brings Dice along. At the meeting, the dialogue between the two chickens, translated from their squawks, reveals a dysfunctional sibling dynamic. Dorothy expresses disdain for Janu’s home and his lifestyle, while Dice stubbornly refuses to return to the farm. After a heated argument involving accusations of racism and personal insults, Dorothy departs, leaving Dice visibly saddened. The event concludes with Janu comforting a dejected Dice as they return home.
