Summary (Click to reveal)

At the start of the sleepover, Janu and Dayai discuss the nature of their relationship and the previous day's events, with Janu teasing Dayai about her anxiety regarding the gathering. Tora arrives first, followed by Tuli, who joins them after finishing extra training. During the preparations, they realize they lack snacks because Dayai left the groceries in her school locker. Janu agrees to go out and buy more, deciding to take Tuli with him to the city center while Tora remains at the apartment with Dayai.

During their excursion, Janu and Tuli have a rare moment of casual conversation. Janu uses the time to ask Tuli about the dynamic between Dayai and Tora, specifically why the two have become inseparable despite their history of one-upmanship. Tuli expresses confusion but notes her appreciation for their newfound bond. Tuli also subtly hints at Tora's romantic inexperience to explain her forward behavior toward Janu. Upon returning to the apartment, they find the tension still high as the group navigates social dynamics and awaits Hana’s arrival. The event concludes with Janu retreating to his room to watch cartoons, leaving the girls to manage the apartment as the sleepover begins in earnest.
